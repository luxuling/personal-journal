The website for our own web and design agency, built together with my friends as we start a service business.

## Why

We wanted a proper home for the studio: one place to explain what we do, show our direction, and make it easy for potential clients to reach us. This project is not just a portfolio page, it is the starting point of a real business we are building together.

## Stack

- **Next.js** — app framework and production deployment flow
- **ReactJS** — component-based UI for pages and sections
- **TailwindCSS** — fast styling system for consistent design

## What's interesting

Because this is for a service business, the website has to do more than look good. Every section is written to communicate value clearly: who we are, what services we offer, and how people can work with us. The focus is clarity, trust, and a clean presentation that reflects our design direction.
